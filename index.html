WHO WE ARE?

Bristol Language School (BLS) is a private language school located in New Damietta City, Egypt. We are committed to providing modern and effective education that combines academic excellence with personal development. Our approach is based on interactive activities that encourage curiosity, independent thinking, and a love for learning.

At BLS, we aim to create a supportive and safe learning environment where every student can reach their full potential. Our mission is to inspire learners to explore, discover, and enjoy education while building strong character, confidence, and positive social values.

We welcome students from pre-kindergarten through secondary levels, and we work closely with each student to understand their individual needs, strengths, and interests. Education at BLS is about more than academics – it's about preparing learners for life with respect, creativity, and leadership skills.
