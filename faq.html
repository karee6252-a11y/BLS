Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions

What is the admission process at Bristol Language School?

The admission process includes the following steps:

  • Complete the online application form on our website
  • Submit required documents (birth certificate, photos, medical records)
  • Attend an interview with the admissions team
  • Receive admission decision within 5-7 working days
  • Complete registration and pay required fees

For more details, please visit our Admission page or contact our admissions office.

What age groups do you accept?

Bristol Language School accepts students from Pre-Kindergarten through Grade 12. We welcome children starting from age 3 up to 18 years old across all educational stages including Kindergarten, Primary, Preparatory, and Secondary levels.

When can I apply for admission?

We accept applications throughout the year. However, we recommend applying early to secure your child's place. The main admission period is typically from March to June for the upcoming academic year starting in September.

What curriculum does Bristol Language School follow?

We follow an internationally recognized curriculum that combines academic excellence with the Egyptian National Curriculum requirements. Our approach focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and global citizenship while respecting local values and culture.

What is the average class size?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student. Our average class size is 20-25 students, allowing teachers to focus on individual learning needs and provide quality education.

Do you offer extra-curricular activities?

Yes! We offer a wide range of extra-curricular activities including:

  • Sports (football, basketball, swimming)
  • Arts and crafts
  • Music and drama
  • Technology and robotics clubs
  • Language clubs

What are the school fees?

School fees vary depending on the grade level. For detailed information about tuition fees and payment plans, please contact our admissions office at 01212881144 or email Bristollanguagesc@gmail.com. We offer flexible payment options to support families.

Are there any additional fees besides tuition?

In addition to tuition, there may be fees for:

  • Registration and admission
  • Books and materials
  • Uniforms
  • Transportation (optional)
  • Extra-curricular activities (optional)

A complete breakdown of all fees will be provided during the admission process.

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are Sunday through Thursday, from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. The school is closed on Fridays and Saturdays. Specific schedules may vary by grade level.

Do you provide school transportation?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable school bus services covering various areas in New Damietta City. Transportation fees are separate from tuition. Please contact our office for routes and availability.

How can parents communicate with teachers?

We encourage open communication between parents and teachers through:

  • Parent-teacher meetings (scheduled throughout the year)
  • Email communication
  • School communication app
  • Phone calls for urgent matters
  • Progress reports sent home regularly

What safety measures are in place?

Student safety is our top priority. We have:

  • Secure campus with controlled entry points
  • CCTV surveillance throughout the school
  • Trained security staff on-site
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Regular safety drills
  • Health clinic with qualified nurse
